Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a shopping list?

Tap the "+" button on the Home screen to create a new list, then add items manually or use the AI recipe generator to auto-fill ingredients.

How does the AI recipe feature work?

Enter a dish name or craving, and Mama's Grocery matches ingredients against your own catalog to build a ready-to-shop list.

How do I remove ads?

Purchase the one-time "Remove Ads" upgrade from the app to unlock an ad-free, unlimited experience.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us at snsd_forlife@hotmail.com with a description of the issue and, if possible, a screenshot.
